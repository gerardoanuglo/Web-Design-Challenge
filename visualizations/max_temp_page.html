Max Temperature

The dataset was assembled by pulling data from the OpenWeatherMap API on over 500 cities, collecting latitude and longitude coordinates and weather information on each city. Using Matplotlib, a scatter plot was created to visualization any correlation between latitude and max temperature record in each city.

Based off this data, latitude and max temperature are correlated. In the sourthern hemisphere, below 0 in latitude, temperature raises as the latitude approaches 0. Max temperature recorded by a city falls as the city latitude moves further north of the equator.
